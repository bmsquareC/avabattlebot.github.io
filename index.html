Meet AVA-X

The ultimate combat robot engineered for high-impact performance and arena dominance

AVA-X Combat Mode

AVA-X is a lightweight combat powerhouse, engineered for high-impact performance and arena dominance. Born from an unrelenting passion for robotics and competition, every component, gear, circuit, and weld has been fine-tuned for precision, speed, and strength.

Designed to strike fast and move faster, AVA-X combines mechanical resilience with intelligent electronics, making it a true force on the battlefield. Whether it's outmaneuvering opponents or delivering powerful blows, AVA-X is always mission-ready.

Technical Specifications

Engineered for maximum performance and destructio

Weapon System

  • Type: Dual Vertical Spinner + Lifter
  • Material: Hardened Steel
  • Speed: 250 MPH tip speed
  • Motor: 2200kv Brushless

Drive Train

  • Type: 4WD, Custom Gearboxes
  • Motors: 4 x High-Torque Brushless
  • Top Speed: 7.6 MPH
  • Wheels: Custom Molded

Armor & Chassis

  • Chassis: Welded 6061 Aluminum
  • Armor: 1/4" HDPE
  • Baseplate: 6061 Aluminum
  • Weight: 14 Kg (Featherweight)
